LITERARY CLUB

About our club

Our club exists to unite people who like to read books and to give them a chance to express themselves. We hold all sorts of events, from poetry evenings to movie hours.

Here you have the opportunity to read a book in sync with a group of people and discuss it with them after at in-person meetings held in our club’s place.

For those who do not live in our city there is an online group with regular zoom sessions. Links will be published under the icons.
